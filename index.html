💡 Sales Tips
🎯
Lead with value, not price
Open with what the customer gains — speed, reliability, savings — before quoting a number.
📱
Always stack XM
Xfinity Mobile is the easiest add-on. Ask "how many lines does your household use?" every call.
🔁
Overcome the "I'll think about it"
Anchor to a specific concern: "What specifically would you want to think over?" — then address it directly.
📅
Schedule before you hang up
A call-back without a time is a dead lead. Lock in a specific day and time before ending the call.
⚡
Use the IE window
Introductory Eligible customers get a .75 CHSI RGU — still a win. Log it correctly and move on.
